## Connectors

- **USB-C:** Power, data, accessory; no standard GPIO breakout.
- **Serial:** Some revisions expose UART for console (see PINE64 wiki).
- **Modem:** Quectel EG25 or similar; AT commands over serial.

No 40-pin or Euler-style header; hardware expansion is via USB or modem. See [PINE64 PinePhone wiki](https://wiki.pine64.org/wiki/PinePhone) and schematic for exact pins.
